About Us

Ideal Skopelos was created in order to help the growing needs of visitors of Skopelos and tourist businesses which have their head office there. Our operation is based on stable partnerships with prominent accommodations of various types, so that the visitors can have multiple options in accordance to their requirements. The choice of our accommodations sets as criteria the highest quality and reliability, giving us the opportunity to offer to our customers the best vacation residences.

Hotels

On our site you can find some of the most remarkable hotels with great benefits. All of them offer unique advantages to the visitor as well as areas of particular natural beauty and close to the sea. The short walking distance from the Town of Skopelos is a key feature in many cases, facilitating the movements during your holidays. You can book on-line directly or send question through the request form.

Policy

The policy of Ideal Skopelos, is based on the connection of the local market with the growing tourist sector of Skopelos. All our partnerships are intended primarily to improve convenience in Skopelos and consequently the emergence of reliable accommodations. In any case, our services do not add extra fees to the interested customer. We are providing you the best prices and at the same time we organize effectively the available services of Skopelos.
